The 247 speakers

The underlying FastSpeech model is conditioned on speaker labels and learns to model 247 different speakers. Here we compare ground truth audio and synthesized audio for a selection of the speakers in the corpus.

Novel voices with pitch target

We can randomly sample from the latent space over speakers to create novel voices. By analyzing the trained embeddings we can determine what "area" in the latent speaker space corresponds with e.g. low pitch or high speaking effort. Here we have sampled 10 new speakers decreasing in pitch.
